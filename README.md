## What Makes Wide Angle Analytics Different?

Wide Angle Analytics takes a fundamentally different approach to web tracking. Instead of relying on invasive cookies that trigger consent requirements, it uses privacy-friendly session identifiers that expire after 24 hours and can't be traced back to individuals. The platform is hosted entirely on European servers by Input Objects GmbH, a German company subject to strict EU data protection laws. Your data never touches third-party servers, and there's no data sharing under any circumstances. All IP addresses are automatically anonymized.

What really sets Wide Angle apart is its resistance to ad blockers. While most analytics tools get blocked by privacy extensions, Wide Angle's technology bypasses these obstacles. Every plan includes the option to use custom domains for tracking, which further improves accuracy. You're seeing actual traffic, not just the portion that makes it through various filters.
